Big Endian: The least significant bit comes last.

0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 | = | 6 | 5 | 4 | 3 | 2 | 1 | 0
--|--|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---
T | E | S | T | I | N | G | = | G | N | I | T | S | E | T


Little Endian: The least significant bit comes first.

0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 | = | 6 | 5 | 4 | 3 | 2 | 1 | 0
--|--|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---
| G | N | I | T | S | E | T | = | T | E | S | T | I | N | G
