#Section A
##1
###a.

Big Endian: The least significant bit comes last.

| 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| T | E | S | T | I | N | G |


Little Endian: The least significant bit comes first.

| 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| G | N | I | T | S | E | T |

###b.

2's Complement (N-bits; N = 4):
| Bit Pattern | 0000 | ... | 0111 | 1000 | ... | 1111 |
| 2's Complement | +0 | ... | 2^(N-1)-1 = 7 | -2^(N-1) = -8 | ... | -1 |
